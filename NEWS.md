
# FlyingR 0.2.3

* Improvements: Zero fat mass in dataset no longer throws an error; throws a 
warning instead and assigns flight range of zero.

* Datasets included:
  * Garden Warblers (garden_wablers) and Lesser Whitethroat (lesser_whitethroats) 
  collected along the southern migration 
routes in autumn.
  * Curlew Sandpiper (curlew_sandpiper) studied and measured under lab conditions.
  
* Documentation: Analysis of the above datasets are included in the README file.
